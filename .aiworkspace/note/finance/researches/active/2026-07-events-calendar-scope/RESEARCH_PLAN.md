# Events Calendar Scope Research

## Why

The Overview Events tab currently shows FOMC, a limited macro set, and provider-estimated earnings. The open question is whether this is enough for stock, macro, and quant investing workflows, especially for S&P 500 and high-quality company earnings.

## Research Question

What event calendars should the product collect so the Events tab answers: "What market background events should I be aware of before interpreting stocks, macro context, and quant results?"

## Scope

- Include market background, scheduled releases, and corporate calendar evidence.
- Exclude trading signals, validation gates, monitoring triggers, broker actions, and automatic action semantics.
- Prefer official sources for government, central bank, exchange, and index calendars.
- Treat corporate earnings dates as provider estimates unless confirmed by company IR or an official filing/source.

## Deliverables

- Current Events coverage gap.
- External calendar benchmark patterns.
- Prioritized collection candidates.
- Implementation guidance for later Events UX phases.
