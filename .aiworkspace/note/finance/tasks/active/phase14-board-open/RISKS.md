# Phase 14 Board Open Risks

Status: Complete
Created: 2026-05-30

## Risks

- Phase 14 could be mistaken for permission to implement all second-cycle candidates at once.
- A high-priority but source-dependent candidate could be started before source feasibility is known.
- The prioritization phase could drift into new persistence or trading automation scope.

## Mitigation

- Keep `phase14-candidate-prioritization-v1` as the immediate next task.
- Separate implementable workflow hardening from research / source review candidates.
- Keep storage and trading automation boundaries explicit in the phase board.
