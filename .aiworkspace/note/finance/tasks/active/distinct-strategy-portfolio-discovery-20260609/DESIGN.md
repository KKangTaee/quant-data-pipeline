# Design

## Constraint

Use each strategy family at most once in the final weighted mix.

Allowed workflow-complete families to prioritize:

- Equal Weight
- GTAA
- Global Relative Strength
- Risk Parity Trend
- Dual Momentum

Research / caveat families:

- Strict annual / quarterly factor variants
- Risk-On Momentum 5D

These can inform the search, but final registration requires Practical Validation and Final Review selected-route eligibility.
