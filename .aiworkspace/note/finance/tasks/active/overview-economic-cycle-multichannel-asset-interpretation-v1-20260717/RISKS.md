# Risks

- Co-movement must not be presented as proven causality.
- Historical reads must not include observations after their reference date.
- The 5-year median materiality threshold may prove too conservative or sensitive in actual UI QA.
- Stages 3-5 remain incomplete after the gold/dollar pilot.
- Stage 3 bonds/rates needs both 2Y/10Y level, curve, real-rate, inflation-expectation, and duration-price paths before a directional card is allowed.
- Stage 4 equities needs earnings/valuation/financial-condition paths; Stage 5 commodities needs a basket rather than crude oil alone.
- Dollar remains `PARTIAL` until comparable foreign-rate data is approved and collected.
- `GC=F` and `DX-Y.NYB` are stored continuous futures proxies, so contract-roll effects can remain in observed price changes.
