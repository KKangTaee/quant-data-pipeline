# RISKS - Product Research Output Contract

Status: Active
Last Updated: 2026-05-13

## Risks

- Reintroducing a `research/` folder can recreate the old catch-all research dump problem if active/done and source contracts are not enforced.
- Product direction research may become stale quickly; volatile external claims need source dates and confidence labels.
- Roadmap changes must still require human approval; research output is evidence, not approval.
